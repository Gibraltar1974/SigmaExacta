1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This report documents the validation testing of the SigmaExacta Weibull Distribution Analysis tool (Excel-based) against JASP (Jeffreys's Amazing Statistics Program). The analysis confirms that SigmaExacta provides identical results to JASP in parameter estimation (Shape and Scale), descriptive statistics, and confidence interval calculations for life data analysis.

2. OBJECTIVE
The objective of this validation was to:
* Verify the accuracy of the Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE) for Weibull parameters.
* Ensure consistency in descriptive statistics and sample characterization.
* Validate the calculation of 95% Confidence Intervals using the Delta Method.
* Confirm that the SigmaExacta tool is a reliable alternative to specialized statistical software like JASP.

* Analysis: Comparison of point estimates, standard errors, and interval bounds.
