What is Sigma Exacta?

In a world where professional tools are often costly and complex, we have created an alternative. We provide you with a comprehensive suite of online tools, created to simplify the most complex challenges in quality and automotive engineering. Forget confusing spreadsheets and prohibitively expensive software. With Sigma Exacta, you get instant and free access to Cpk calculators, FMEA templates, and DOE analysis, among many others. All of this, with precision and rigor. Discover how precision becomes accessible and quality becomes your best tool.

About the Author

Javier Casal Gómez (Spain) is an Industrial Engineer (Universidad de Vigo) with over two decades of professional experience in the transportation sector. He owns a Master in Numerical Simulation (UNED) and an MBA in automotive business (PSA Peugeot Citroën). Throughout his career, he has participated in numerous projects at top-tier multinational companies such as Stellantis, Magna International, and Mahle. It was during this journey that he identified a clear need in the market: accessible, quality tools. The existing options were limited to spreadsheets or specialized software that was both costly and complex. With the mission to simplify engineering and make precision accessible to everyone, he created Sigma Exacta. Every tool found here is a reflection of his commitment to technical excellence, designed to be intuitive and precise.
