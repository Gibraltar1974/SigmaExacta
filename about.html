What is Sigma Exacta?

In a world where professional tools are often costly, opaque, and complex, Sigma Exacta offers a different path. We provide a comprehensive suite of online tools designed to simplify the most intricate challenges in innovation, design, development, and quality control.

Currently, users have instant and free access to our core engines, including Capability calculators, FMEA builders, and TRIZ analysis, among many others. Discover how precision becomes accessible and quality becomes your best tool.

Precision for Everyone, Control for You

We believe your data belongs to you. That’s why our platform is built on a "local-first" philosophy: all calculations run directly in your browser. By processing data on your side, we ensure:

  • Maximum Privacy: Your sensitive industrial data never leaves your device.
  • Zero Latency: Instant results without server-side bottlenecks.
  • Data Sovereignty: You maintain full authority over your information.

Commitment to Open Standards

Forget the "vendor lock-in" of prohibitively expensive software or rigid, proprietary spreadsheets. We are moving away from closed formats like Excel in favor of free and open standards, ensuring your work remains accessible and interoperable forever.

Roadmap: The Future of Sigma Exacta

We are constantly evolving. While our calculation engines are fully operational, we are planning the following future milestones to further empower our community (currently in the planning stage):

  • Knowledge Without Barriers (Creative Commons): We are preparing a library of CC-licensed video tutorials. Our goal is to ensure that not just the tools, but the methodologies themselves, are accessible to everyone as a public good.
  • Verified Precision: We are working toward a rigorous validation of all mathematical models to ensure the highest standards of accuracy for industrial and professional use.
  • Full Autonomy: A future update will enhance our local-first approach by allowing you to save and load your projects locally, keeping your workflow independent of any cloud server.
  • Desktop Experience: We are aiming to develop a Progressive Web App (PWA) to provide the seamless, offline-capable experience of desktop software with the agility of the web.

About the Author

Javier Casal Gómez (Spain) is an Industrial Engineer (Universidad de Vigo) with over two decades of professional experience in the transportation sector. He owns a Master in Numerical Simulation (UNED) and a MBA in automotive business (PSA Peugeot Citroën). Throughout his career, he has participated in numerous projects at top-tier multinational companies such as Stellantis, Magna International, and Mahle. It was during this journey that he identified a clear need in the market: accessible, quality tools. The existing options were limited to spreadsheets or specialized software that was both costly and complex. With the mission to simplify engineering and make precision accessible to everyone, he created Sigma Exacta. Every tool found here is a reflection of his commitment to technical excellence, designed to be intuitive and precise.
