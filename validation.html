Sigma Exacta is committed to transparency and accuracy in all engineering calculations. This section provides comprehensive validation data comparing our tools against established reference software, industry standards, and validated datasets. Our goal is to ensure that engineers can trust the results provided by Sigma Exacta for critical decision-making.

Validation Methodology

Our Verification Approach

All Sigma Exacta tools undergo rigorous validation using the following methodology:

  • Reference Software Comparison: Results are compared against established engineering software
  • Published Dataset Verification: Testing against validated datasets from academic and industry sources
  • Real-world Scenario Testing: Application to actual engineering problems

All validation tests are documented with complete datasets, methodologies, and results to ensure reproducibility.
