What is the PDCA Cycle?

The PDCA Cycle (Plan‑Do‑Check‑Act), also known as the Deming Wheel or Shewhart Cycle, is a systematic, iterative four‑stage approach for continuous improvement in processes and products.

1. Plan

Identify goal & plan action.

2. Do

Implement the plan.

4. Act

Standardize or adjust.

3. Check

Analyze the results.

History & Origin

The concept was originally developed by Walter A. Shewhart and later popularized by W. Edwards Deming.
